Where is Jakarta?
JAKARTA'S TRAVEL DESTINATION

DKI Jakarta

Daerah Khusus Ibukota Jakarta (DKI Jakarta) is the capital city of the Republic of Indonesia. It is located in the northwest of Java Island. DKI Jakarta is the largest metropolis in Southeast Asia. This city is divided into five districts: Central Jakarta, North Jakarta, West Jakarta, East Jakarta, and South Jakarta. The city that has been around for 494 years has many stories, both from the dense population, traffic jams, and many other issues. Despite having various issues, Jakarta is still in demand by all levels of society, whether a place of making a living, shopping, and traveling. Talking about shopping, Jakarta is the best place to visit because the goods provided are complete and the prices offered are relatively cheap. Some famous shopping places that can be used as options to visit if you are in Jakarta, such as ITC Mangga Dua, Pasar Tanah Abang, Pasar Senen, Pasar Baru, and many more. Besides that, related to the travel destination, although it is difficult to find natural tourist destinations in Jakarta, some man-made tourist attractions are certainly no less attractive. Some of the recommended tourist attractions have been explained for you.

South Jakarta

Gelora Bung Karno

Gelanggang Olahraga (Gelora) Bung Karno is a multi-purpose sports complex in Senayan, Jakarta, Indonesia. It is the largest sports complex in Indonesia. This sports complex is a popular place for people to do physical exercises like running, walking, bicycling, etc. Besides being used for sports activities, GBK can also be used for business activities and recreation activities. In addition, the Gelora Bung Karno area is also used as the Green Open Area, which contains various large and shady rare trees. Therefore, you will hear the beautiful chirping of 22 types of wild birds and colorful koi fish that can relieve your stress.

Central Jakarta

MRT

Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) is a fast rail transportation system in Jakarta. The Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) project is an infrastructure project that aims to relieve great traffic congestion in Indonesia's capital city. The currently operating track is a 15.7 km long track that connects Lebak Bulus Station with the Bundaran HI Station. The initial fare charged is Rp. 3,000 as the minimum fare and increases by Rp. 1,000 each time you pass the station. The highest fare is Rp. 14,000, which is a trip from Lebak Bulus Station to Bundaran HI Station.

East Jakarta

Taman Mini Indonesia Indah

Traveling to Jakarta is not complete if you haven't visited Taman Mini Indonesia Indah (TMII). Taman Mini Indonesia Indah is an Indonesian cultural-themed tourist park in East Jakarta. This park is the overview of the culture of the Indonesians, which includes various aspects of the daily life of the people of 33 provinces of Indonesia. It also displays various clothing, dances, and regional traditions. Besides that, in the middle of TMII there is a lake that portrays a miniature of the Indonesian archipelago, a cable car, various museums, and the Keong Mas IMAX Theater and Tanah Airku Theater. These various recreational facilities make Taman Mini Indonesia Indah (TMII) one of Jakarta's leading tourist areas.

West Jakarta

Central Park

As mentioned before, one best thing to do in Jakarta is shopping. There are so many great shopping places in Jakarta, one of them is Central Park. It is one of the biggest malls in Jakarta. This shopping center is located in a complex with office buildings, hotels, and apartments. This mall is located between Taman Anggrek Mall and Ciputra Mall. For those who want to shop, Central Park might be a good choice to find what you are looking for.

North Jakarta

Ancol Beach

Ancol Dreamland Park is the largest and most comprehensive theme park in Jakarta. Located in North Jakarta facing the Java Sea, Taman Impian Jaya Ancol is a beachfront resort area that stands on an area of 80 hectares. This is a recreational park that provides various facilities, from water and beach sports, fun rides, swimming pools, to an Eco-Park and international caliber star-studded spectacular shows.
